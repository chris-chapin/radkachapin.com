Trauma
 I help people who have experienced sexual, physical, and emotional abuse, domestic violence and violent crimes as well as people who suffer from PTSD.  Trauma can make us feel lonely and isolated amidst a crowd. It can impact our sense of self and take control over our lives.  My approach to treating trauma uses Lifespan Integration Therapy which is a new, gentle body-based model of therapy that facilitates rapid healing without re-traumatizing.

Intimate Partner Violence
 Those impacted by intimate partner violence often find themselves stuck in a very painful situation with their self-esteem and self-identity shattered.  You may be experiencing fear, shame, anger, and guilt.  Isolation is a very common strategy employed by batterers and your world may be getting smaller and smaller as a result of your partner's demands.  Whether you are in a heterosexual or same-sex relationship, I can help you navigate this complex situation.

LGBT
 I help people who identify as sexual and gender minorities, whether their issues are related to LGBT status or not.  I offer you a safe, friendly space where you can embrace who you are.

Relationships
 The templates of how we relate to others are created very early on in life.  With these templates, we may find ourselves walking around others on eggshells in order to avert another person's anger. We may adopt pleasing, caretaking, and enabling - or even avoiding relationships altogether - as strategies to protect ourselves from abandonment. The very same strategies that we have learned to use to protect ourselves from early childhood wounds can become obstacles to growth later in life. Together, we'll work on making your relationships places of healing and growth, where you can freely be yourself.

Addiction
 Being affected by somebody else's addiction is emotionally draining.  It often makes you feel like you are crazy or the one with the problem.  There is a lot of insanity in addiction.  If you have been neglecting yourself, I can help you focus on your own well-being and finding a sense of internal balance.

Immigration
 Changing countries and leaving your culture behind is no easy task.  Trying to find the right balance between fitting in and preserving your cultural identity is challenging.  Together, we'll explore how you can celebrate your diversity and make it a strength that you can build on.
