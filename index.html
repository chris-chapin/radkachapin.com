Do you have problems with relationships? Are your relationships unsafe and scary? Do you find yourself repeating the same patterns? You may have experienced too many losses and disappointments to allow yourself to be vulnerable anymore.

Welcome. You have taken the first step by noticing that things are not going the way you want and have decided to reach out for help. It may have been a long process to get here.

Please know this – you are not broken. You have done everything that’s supposed to work. You have been trying to figure this out for a long time and maybe while this is happening, you have put your life on hold while you are stuck with the present situation.
